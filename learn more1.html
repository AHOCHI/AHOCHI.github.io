2015

2015: study towards integrating Pygoogle and Scrapy to Facilitate Geographically Constrained Searches.This systeem provides the help to any social worker in the field could quickly and easily inform their clients of the closest institutions that would be able to meet their needs. Some examples of types of support would be a food bank or free clinic. Upon completion and implementation, this project will greatly optimize the efficiency of clients being able to locate social services that are relevant to their needs and geographically near. programer were able to integrate two powerful programs: Pygoogle and Scrapy.Pygoogle is a program designed to take a word or phrase from the user and return the top google results. Scrapy is a web scraper. This means that when sent to a website, it will collect and organize data. When used together, Pygoogle passes a list of websites to Scrapy, which then extracts specific information from each one. This information is used to give the user an idea of which website will best give them what they are looking for, as well as to give the rest of the program data to help organize results.
