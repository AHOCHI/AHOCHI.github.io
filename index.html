AHOCHI

AHOCHI is a proposed Humanitarian Free and Open Source Software (HFOSS) project that allows social workers to perform geographically constrained searches on behalf of their clients. This would mean that a social worker in the field could quickly and easily inform their clients of the closest institutions that would be able to meet their needs. Some examples of types of support would be a food bank or free clinic. & etc
